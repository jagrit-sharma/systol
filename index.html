Your heartbeat, live in the browser.

Systol turns a browser tab into a bedside monitor for your workouts, your recovery, or just your resting beat. Connect a Bluetooth chest strap or a compatible watch to see your live BPM, zones, and vitals.

No sensor? Try the demo first.

Your data never leaves your device.

2 · WatchLive BPM, a scrolling trace, your time in each zone, and alerts when you hit a target.
